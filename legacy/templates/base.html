Subcreation Mythic+ provides statistical analysis of Mythic+ dungeons in World of Warcraft, using the raider.io API, and provides summaries of the top talents, essences, azerite, gems, and gear used in Mythic+, using the Warcraft Logs API. Item and ability tooltips by Wowhead. All images copyright Blizzard Entertainment.

Subcreation Mythic+ updates every eight hours for top M+ runs (via raider.io) and every 24 hours for top talents, essences, azerite, gems, and gear (via Warcraft Logs).
